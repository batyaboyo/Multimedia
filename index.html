What is Multimedia?

Multimedia is content that uses a combination of different forms of content:

Text • Audio • Images • Animations • Video • Interactive content

Components of Multimedia

• Text: Written content

• Images: Pictures, graphics, photographs

• Audio: Sound, music, narration

• Video: Moving images

• Animation: Animated graphics

Uses of Multimedia

• Education and E-learning

• Entertainment

• Business presentations

• Marketing and advertising

• Social media

• Websites and Apps

Benefits of Multimedia

• Enhanced engagement

• Better understanding

• Improved retention

• Multiple learning styles

• Interactive experience
